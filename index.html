About Me


I was a Computer Science masters student studying at the University of Birmingham. I have just finished my MSc in Advanced Computer Science there.

I am interested in research in Theoretical Computer Science, particularly in Programming Language Semantics. My masters project studied the Operational and Denotational Semantics of PCF and the Adequacy theorem for it.

I enjoy programming in many languages, my personal favourite being Haskell, but I am most experienced in Java! I have also used Agda, OCaml, Javascript and Python, and have attempted C and C++ (and hope to master low level programming one day!)

When not doing technological things I enjoy playing the Violin and Viola and am a member of a lovely local amateur orchestra in Birmingham. Incidentally, if you play either of these instruments, we're desparate for more strings, so take a look here!

In my local community, I am a STEMNET ambassador and a member of the South West Birmingham Liberal Democrats.
