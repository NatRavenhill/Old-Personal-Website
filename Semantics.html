Formalising the Operational and Denotational Semantics of Programming Languages

This was a Mini Project for my masters degree, which I am cuurently working on.

I have been interested in Semantics for a while and after trying out Axiomatic Semantics in my last mini project, I wanted to learn more about the other two types of Semantics.

I started with a very simple imperative langauge, like the While languages found in introductory Semantics textbooks. My language is a simple imperative langauge with variable assignment, conditionals and repetition.

In order to make my proofs and definitions more interesting, I chose to formalise my work in the proof assistant Agda. So far I have specified the language, its denotational and operational semantics and the proof that the operational semantics are sound and complete with relation to the denotational semantics.
